INSTRUCTIONS ON HOW TO CHECK OUT A KEY


You can see how many keys are available for each room on the right side of this
page. If you would like to make a key request, click on any of the blocks
and you will see a request button if there are keys available to request.
Press the request button and fill in the relative information. You will
be notified by email once your request has been approved or denied.

Alternatively you may also navigate to the All Available Keys catalog
in the menu section on the left. There you will see a list of keys that are
currently available. Click the request button on the right to request for
a key.
